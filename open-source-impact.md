# Open-Source Impact

Industrial quality teams in small and mid-sized companies often depend on fragmented spreadsheets, manual records, PDFs, and disconnected workflows.

Large enterprise systems such as ERP, MES, QMS, and BI platforms can be expensive, complex, and difficult to implement for smaller teams.

This project aims to provide an open-source foundation for a lighter alternative.

## Intended Impact

The project helps industrial teams:

- Structure quality control data.
- Improve traceability.
- Detect recurring failure modes.
- Connect defects with products and operations.
- Generate faster quality reports.
- Prepare for audits.
- Use AI to support operational decision-making.

## Why Open Source

An open-source foundation allows teams, students, developers, quality managers, and industrial engineers to experiment with practical quality analytics workflows without starting from zero.

The project can also serve as an educational reference for:

- Industrial data modeling.
- Quality management workflows.
- Dashboard design.
- AI-assisted operational analysis.
- Lightweight SaaS architecture.
